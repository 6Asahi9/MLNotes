### **Step 1: Pick two vectors**

### **Step 2: Formula for inner product**

[
\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{b} = a_1 b_1 + a_2 b_2 + a_3 b_3

### **Step 3: Multiply corresponding components**

---

### **Step 4: Add them up**

[
4 + (-6) + 5 = 3
]

---

### ‚úÖ **Answer**

[
\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{b} = 3
]
